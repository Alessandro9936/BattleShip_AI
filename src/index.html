Welcome Player!

Before starting a new game you need to place your ships on the board. Rules for placing ships: Place each ship in any horizontal or vertical position. Do not place a ship so that any part of it over-laps the edge of the grid or another ship.

You can place your ships by clicking on the board. a preview will be displayed.

You can also change the placing direction of your ships.

Click on the enemy board to send a shot, you will see a mark on the cell you clicked. If the mark is red you hit a ship otherwise if it is blue your shot is a miss.

You are playing against a computer, its shots are random. Rule 1 apply also for your board when the computer send a shot. Computer is not smart, it will be an easy win...or not?

The legend below contains all your ships, whenever a ship is sunked it will turn to red.

To win and end the game you need to hit and sunk all 5 computer's ships.
